Data Processing

The data will be fed into the code such that:

  • The data in January 2022 have been extract from the spreadsheet within the dataset provided (URL: https://data.london.gov.uk/dataset/the-rising-cost-of-living-and-its-effects-on-londoners).
  • The data in May 2022, September 2022 and January 2023 have been taken from the 3 corresponding spreadsheets inside the GLA cost of living polling dataset (URL: https://data.london.gov.uk/dataset/gla-poll-results-cost-of-living-2022).

5 percentage values in total at the "All" column will be taken as categories' display of proportion of sample population using a specific way of managing living costs across all 4 spreadsheets. This will be reflected as the length of a specific bar inside the grouped bar chart. More details regarding how those values have been extracted and processed can be seen in the "Implementation" section of the Miro board (URL: https://miro.com/app/board/uXjVPnH-TAU=/?share_link_id=479439485808).
